Help

Welcome to the help page for Join, your guide to using our kanban project management tool.

What is Join?

Join is a kanban-based project management tool designed and built by a group of dedicated students as part of their web development bootcamp at the Developer Akademie.

Kanban, a Japanese term meaning "billboard," is an effective method to visualize work, limit work-in-progress, and maximize efficiency. Join leverages these principles to help users manage their tasks and projects in an intuitive, visual interface.

Note: Join is an educational exercise and not intended for extensive business usage. While we strive for the best user experience, we cannot guarantee consistent availability, reliability, or accuracy.

How to Use Join

Exploring the Board

When you log in, you’ll see a default board with four lists: "To Do", "In Progress", "Await Feedback", and "Done".

Creating Contacts

Go to the "Contacts" section, click "New contact", and fill in the details. You can assign tasks to contacts.

Adding Cards

Click the "+" button under a list to create a new task card. Add details like task name, description, due date, and assignees.

Moving Cards

Drag and drop cards between lists to update their status or use the button in the top-right corner of a card.

Deleting Cards

Move completed tasks to "Done" or delete them permanently.

For effective use, ensure that you and your team update the board regularly.
